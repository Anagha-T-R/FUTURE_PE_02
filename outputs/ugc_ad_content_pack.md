## UGC Script 1 - Instagram Reels

**Angle:** Sensitive skin and simple routine  
**Length:** 30 seconds  
**Format:** Creator speaking to camera with product demo

### Spoken Script

"Sensitive skin people, this one is for us. I used to keep adding more products whenever my skin felt dry or irritated, but honestly, that made it worse. Now I keep my morning routine simple: cleanser, GlowNest Barrier Repair Gel Cream, and sunscreen. This has a lightweight gel texture, so it does not feel heavy, but my skin still feels comfortable after it sinks in. I like it especially when my skin feels tight after washing. If your routine is doing too much, try simplifying it with one moisturizer your skin actually likes."

### Visual Direction

- Creator shows bare face after cleansing.
- Close-up of product texture on fingertips.
- Apply gel cream on cheek and forehead.
- Show sunscreen going on smoothly after moisturizer.
- End with product beside cleanser and sunscreen.

### On-Screen Text

- "Sensitive skin routine"
- "Cleanser + barrier cream + SPF"
- "Lightweight, not greasy"
- "Simple skincare wins"

### CTA

"Try GlowNest if your skin needs a calmer daily routine."

## Content Testing Plan

| Test | Variable | Goal |
|---|---|---|
| Hook test | Sensitive skin vs sticky moisturizer | Identify strongest opening angle |
| CTA test | "Shop today" vs "Save this routine" | Compare purchase intent and saves |
| Platform test | Instagram vs YouTube Shorts | Compare watch-through rate |
| Visual test | Texture close-up vs routine demo | Learn which proof style builds trust |

## Final Notes

This content pack focuses on realistic product benefits, creator-style delivery, and low-friction CTAs. The scripts avoid exaggerated skincare promises and keep the message simple enough for a real creator to film.
